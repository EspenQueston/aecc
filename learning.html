Recommended Online Courses

Enhance your skills with these carefully selected online courses that will help you excel in your academic and professional journey.

Introduction to Chinese Culture

8 weeks Beginner English/French

This comprehensive course introduces students to the rich cultural heritage of China, covering history, philosophy, arts, customs, and modern society. Perfect for international students looking to better understand their host country.

Enroll Now

Academic Writing in English

6 weeks Intermediate English

Develop essential academic writing skills for research papers, essays, and theses. This course covers structure, argumentation, citation styles, and language conventions required for scholarly writing at international universities.

Research Methods for Graduate Students

Master the research process from hypothesis formulation to data analysis and interpretation. This course equips graduate students with the methodological tools needed for successful thesis and dissertation research.
