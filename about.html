Our Mission & Vision

The Association of Congolese Students in China (AECC) is dedicated to supporting Congolese students throughout their academic journey in China. We aim to create a strong community that fosters academic excellence, cultural exchange, and professional development.

Community

Building a supportive network for all Congolese students across China

Excellence

Promoting academic achievement and professional growth

Partnership

Fostering Sino-Congolese relations through cultural exchange

Our Vision

To be the premier platform that connects, empowers, and represents Congolese students in China, facilitating their success and promoting Sino-Congolese relations through education, cultural exchange, and professional development.
