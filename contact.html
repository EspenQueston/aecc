Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member of AECC?

To become a member, you need to be a Congolese student currently studying in China. Register through our website by clicking the "Login" button and then "Register here". After completing the registration form, your application will be reviewed, and you'll receive a confirmation email within 48 hours.

What benefits do I get as an AECC member?

As a member, you'll gain access to networking opportunities, cultural events, academic resources, scholarship information, and career counseling. You'll also receive regular updates on important news and events relevant to Congolese students in China.

Is there a membership fee?

No, membership to AECC is free for all eligible Congolese students studying in China. Our association is funded through donations, partnerships, and sponsors.

How can I get involved in AECC activities?

Once you become a member, you'll receive invitations to all our events and activities. You can also volunteer for committees, contribute to our blog, participate in forums, or propose new initiatives. Contact us directly if you have specific interests or skills you'd like to contribute.

How can I request assistance with academic or visa issues?

Members can request assistance by filling out the contact form on this page, sending an email to support@aecc.org, or reaching out through WeChat. Please provide a detailed description of your issue, and our support team will respond within 24-48 hours.
