About AECC

The Association of Congolese Students in China (AECC) is a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting Congolese students throughout their academic journey in China. We provide resources, community support, and opportunities for professional development.

Founded in 2010, our association has grown to include over 500 members across various universities in China. We organize cultural events, academic workshops, and networking opportunities to help our members thrive in their studies and beyond.

Academic Support

Access resources, study groups, and mentorship programs to excel in your academic pursuits. Our network of experienced students and alumni provides guidance on course selection, research opportunities, and academic challenges.
